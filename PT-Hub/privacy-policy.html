Privacy Policy

PT Hub

This Privacy Policy describes how PT Hub ("we", "us", or "the App") handles information when you use our Android application. PT Hub is a client-management tool for personal trainers. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

In short: PT Hub is an offline app. Everything you enter — your clients, sessions, workouts and measurements — is stored only on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no access to your data. The only data that leaves your device is what Google collects to serve ads and process purchases.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data You Provide

The App allows you to record and manage your personal-training business, including:

  • Client records: name, age, height, weight, training goals, notes and tags
  • Body measurements: chest, waist, hips, arms, thighs, neck and related notes
  • Progress entries: weight and body-fat history, personal records
  • Exercises and workout templates: exercise library, sets, reps, weights and supersets
  • Sessions: scheduled dates and times, assigned client, status and session notes

All of this data is stored locally on your device using an on-device database (Room/SQLite). We do not transmit, collect, or have access to it on any server.

1.2 Data About Your Clients

Because PT Hub is a professional tool, the records you create may contain personal information about other people — your training clients. That information is entered by you and stays on your device; it is never sent to us or to anyone else by the App.

You are responsible for the client information you record. Where data-protection laws such as the GDPR apply, you act as the controller of your clients' data and we act as neither controller nor processor, since we never receive it. You should ensure you have an appropriate legal basis (for example, your clients' consent) for recording their details, keep your device secured, and honour any request from a client to see or delete their record — the App lets you edit or delete any client and all of their associated data at any time.

1.3 App Preferences

The App stores your preferences locally on your device, including:

  • Display theme (dark, light or system)
  • Unit system (metric or imperial)
  • App language
  • Session reminder and backup reminder times
  • Whether the ad-free purchase has been made

These preferences are stored using Android DataStore and never leave your device.

1.4 Information Collected by Third-Party Services

The App integrates third-party services that may independently collect certain information. See Section 3 for details.

2. How We Use Information

Your locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Display your client list, client details and session history
  • Show your schedule, dashboard statistics and progress charts
  • Build and reuse workout templates
  • Show local reminders for upcoming sessions, missed sessions and backups
  • Export your data to PDF or Excel files saved to your device
  • Create and restore local backup files saved to your device

We do not use your data for analytics, advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the App's core functionality to you.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

The free version of the App displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process certain data in accordance with Google's privacy practices, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. Your client, session and measurement data is never shared with AdMob. You can learn more in the Google Privacy Policy and manage your ad preferences in your device's Settings > Google > Ads.

3.2 Google Play Billing

The App offers an optional one-time in-app purchase that removes advertisements. Purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. We never see or store your payment details — the App only receives confirmation from Google Play that a purchase was made, which is stored locally on your device. Google's handling of purchase data is described in the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local storage only: All your data and preferences are stored exclusively on your device.
  • No cloud sync: The App does not upload your data to any cloud service or remote server.
  • No account required: The App does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or sign in.
  • Backup and export files: When you use the backup or export features, a JSON, Excel or PDF file is saved to a location you choose on your device. These files contain your client data in readable form. You are responsible for the security of these files and for anyone you share them with.
  • Device backup: Android may include the App's data in the device backup provided by your operating system. This is controlled by your Android and Google account settings, not by us.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The App itself does not use cookies or tracking technologies. However, Google AdMob may use advertising identifiers and similar technologies to serve and measure ads. These are managed by Google and subject to Google's policies.

The App does not use any analytics or crash-reporting SDKs (such as Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics or Crashlytics) and does not track your usage behaviour.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data, your client data, or any other App data with any third party. The only data that leaves your device is:

  • Information collected by Google AdMob for ad serving
  • Purchase information processed by Google Play Billing
  • Standard network requests required for the above services (requiring the INTERNET permission)

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is a professional tool intended for use by personal trainers and fitness professionals.

If you record data about a minor client, you are responsible for obtaining consent from their parent or legal guardian in accordance with applicable law.

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and correct your data: All data is visible and editable inside the App at any time.
  • Delete your data: You can delete individual clients, sessions or records in the App, wipe all data from the Settings screen, or remove everything by clearing the App's data in your device settings or uninstalling the App. Deleting a client also deletes their measurements, progress entries and personal records.
  • Export your data: You can export your data to PDF or Excel format, or create a full JSON backup.
  • Remove ads: You can make the optional one-time in-app purchase to remove advertisements.
  • Opt out of personalized ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).
  • Notifications: You can turn reminders off in the App's Settings screen or revoke the notification permission in your device settings.

9. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET: Required for loading advertisements and for Google Play Billing.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Used to show local session, missed-session and backup reminders on your device.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Used to re-schedule your reminders after the device restarts.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Used by the background scheduler that delivers those reminders.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below) / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 10 and below): Used for saving exported and backup files to device storage on older Android versions.

All reminders are generated and delivered locally on your device. No reminder content is sent over the internet.
